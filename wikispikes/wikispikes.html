Data: Wikimedia Pageviews API (user traffic, all platforms). Spike = views on the selected day divided by the median of the 29 days before it, computed for the day's top 50 most-viewed articles. Picking a country instead analyzes that country's own top Wikipedia articles (by reader location, in their local language). Picking a category scans a wider pool of top articles, classifies them via Wikidata, and keeps only that category; thin categories (e.g. AI) are topped up with a list of notable articles so each still ranks a full set by spike. Each day / category / country selection shows its top 20 spikes. Pageview data is published with about one day of delay.
